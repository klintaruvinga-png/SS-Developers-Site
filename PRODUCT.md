# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

General visitors seeking information about the site.

## Product Purpose

Provide an engaging, informative online presence that clearly conveys the brand's offerings and values to visitors.

## Brand Personality

Bold, Innovative, Reliable

## Anti-references

_None_

## Design Principles

- **Show, don't tell** – use visual hierarchy and imagery to convey messages quickly.
- **Practice what you preach** – ensure the design itself embodies confidence and reliability.
- **Expert confidence** – present information with clear, authoritative typography and layout.
- **Inclusive clarity** – maintain high contrast and readable text for all users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance for contrast and navigation.
- Consider color‑blind safe palettes and provide reduced‑motion alternatives.
